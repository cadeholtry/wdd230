WDD 230: The Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Folsom Chamber of Commerce. This name represents the city in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

The overall goal of the chamber of commerce site is to promote the business interests of the members of the organization and promote the area, attracting additional business, tourism, and people. The site will include things such as upcoming events for the city as well as spotlights for members of the chamber.

Scenarios

  1. What is the weather like on event days?
  2. Where can I go to have my event listed on the site?
  3. Which buisnesses are part of the chamber?

Color Schema

I will be using a few different colors for my site. A nice cheery yellow (#f0e546) for headers, and section title backgrounds, A dark grey (#201E20) for navigation and text color, and a lighter grey (#f0f0f0) for information card backgrounds. The standard website background will be white.

Typography

I will be using Montserrat for all font uses, as it has a lighter, modern feel while being very readable and pleasent to look at.
