Join the Chamber Today!

Desired Membership Level:

Gold Membership ($5,000+)

Gold Membership holders gain all benefits, and are first in line for booth spots, newsletter pages, and other advertisement opprotunities. Gold members also gain insight into uncoming events before other members, allowing for better preperation for them.

Silver Membership ($1,000+)

Silver Members gain all the benefits of a Bronze and NP Member, along with potential to set up booths at events, entire pages in the newsletter, and additional advertisement in the city area.

Bronze Membership ($100+)

Bronze Members get all the benefits of the Non-Profit Members as well as additional benefits. These include free swag and newsletters monthly. Features in these newsletters may also happen.

Non-Profit Membership

Holders of the NP Membership will get their company or business advertised in their own category on our website and events, as well as potential features in our monthly newsletters!
